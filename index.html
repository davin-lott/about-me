My Personal Interests

  One of my big interests is stories. I love watching movies and TV shows, reading books, and talking about those stories with others. I really love seeing how stories are put together, and the tricks that the authors/creators have to make us feel certain ways or to set things up. I like almost all genres for stories. I'll watch anything from horror to rom-coms.

  Another one of my interests is juggling. My aunt and uncle taught me the basics of juggling when I was almost twelve, and I pretty much taught myself from there. I have juggled eight juggling balls, performed a number of times, and love creating new tricks. It is also a good work out, though I have been injured by it.

  The last interest I'll talk about is solving logic problems. I love the problem-solving that happens with Computer Science. When I was in high school I figured out how to make my own Spot-It game, which is where every card in a deck has one and only one match with every other card in the deck.
